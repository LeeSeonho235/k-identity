What Makes a Korean Name?

🀄 Hanja Names (한자 이름)

Most Korean names are made of two Chinese characters called Hanja (한자). Each character carries a distinct meaning — for example, 旻 means "sky" and 俊 means "talented." The combination creates a unique identity with deep cultural roots.

🌿 Pure Korean Names (순우리말)

Pure Korean names (순우리말) use native Korean words instead of Hanja. Names like 하늘 (sky) or 빛나 (shining) are growing in popularity as parents embrace Korea's native linguistic heritage.

🎭 K-Drama Style

Korean drama characters have names that feel both beautiful and meaningful — like 서준 (Seo-jun) or 민아 (Min-ah). Our AI draws from this naming culture to give you a name that truly sounds Korean.
